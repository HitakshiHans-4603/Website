TUFF-12.
2019
View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Team Fateh (@fateh_fsae)
TUFF-12.
Participating in Formula Student Lincoln and Formula Bharat, we knocked the ball out of the park. Backed with the new KTM 390cc engine, we also managed to make our car 20kg lighter! During Formula Student Lincoln, we achieved all the tech stickers. The tech stickers included the technical inspection, tilt test, noise test, and brake test. Not only that, we were the ONLY Indian team to run in the endurance event. We ranked 67th overall during our time in Lincoln. In Formula Bharat, the team secured an overall rank of 29th. Yet again, we made sure our car was cost-efficient securing, fairly good ranks in the cost and manufacturing event. In 2019 we were also fortunate enough to run 500km in the pre-testing phase. It was one of our better performances, bagging such achievements we were very keen to improve more and come back stronger.

GARUDA
Participating in Formula Student Germany in 2013. It was our lightest car to date! , coming in at 180 kg. Not only that, we were the first Indian team to use an Aluminium Honeycomb Monocoque chassis. ASTRA
The year 2012 marked our fifth entry to the Formula Student. A commemorative year stood for a car just as unforgettable, featuring the ASTRA. TUFF-01
The TUFF-01 our first attempt at making a car from the ground up.
